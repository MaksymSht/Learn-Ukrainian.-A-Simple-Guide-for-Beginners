Important

Additional explanation for Ь (Soft Sign):

The soft sign (Ь) has no direct equivalent in English or Portuguese. It’s not pronounced on its own but indicates that the preceding consonant should be softened. Think of it as a signal to make the previous sound gentler. For example, in Ukrainian words like м’ята (mint) or сіль (salt), the letters м or с are pronounced more softly than usual.

Simple Phrases

Привіт! – Hello!

Як справи? – How are you?

Мене звати… – My name is…

Дякую – Thank you

Будь ласка – Please / You’re welcome

Так / Ні – Yes / No

Я з Бразилії – I’m from Brazil

До побачення!

– Goodbye!

Вибачте. – Excuse me / Sorry.

Я люблю тебе. – I love you.
